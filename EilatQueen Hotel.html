Queen of Sheba Eilat Hotel At the heart of Eilat where the crystal Red Sea and the Desert mountains meet, stands the Queen of Sheba Eilat Hotel . Situated in a prime position on the sea front, the hotel offers breathtaking views of the Red Sea, the mountains and the beautiful lagoon. The Queen of Sheba Eilat Hotel is designed as the imaginary biblical palace of the Queen of Sheba, the architecture and interior design are characterized by hand painted motifs, mosaics and domes. The hotel offers a spacious synagogue, energetic activities and a childrens club.
