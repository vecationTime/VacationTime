welcome to the EilatLagona Hotel

Isrotel Lagoona is an excellent choice for travelers visiting Eilat, offering a family-friendly environment alongside many helpful amenities designed to enhance your stay. Close to some of Eilat's most popular landmarks, such as Red Sea Yacht (0.0 mi) and Harbour Bridge (0.1 mi), Isrotel Lagoona All Inclusive Hotel is a great destination for tourists. Isrotel Lagoona is a family-friendly all-inclusive resort offering a flat screen TV, a minibar, and air conditioning in the rooms, and it is easy to stay connected during your stay as free wifi is offered to guests. The all-inclusive resort features a concierge. Plus, guests can enjoy a pool and breakfast, which have made this a popular choice among travelers visiting Eilat. For guests with a vehicle, free parking is available. While in Eilat, you may want to check out some of the restaurants that are a short walk away from Isrotel Lagoona, including 5th Avenue (0.3 mi), Eddie's Hideaway (1.1 mi), and Whale (0.4 mi). There is no shortage of things to do in the area: explore popular history museums such as Eilat, My City History Museum. Enjoy your stay in Eilat!
